Who we are:

Welcome to our website!

Discover the fascinating history of Inflight Sales Group (ISG), a company that has been at the forefront of providing exceptional services to airlines since its inception in 1982. Founded by the visionary Jean-Marcel Rouff, ISG's predecessor was the original concessionaire, supplying amenity kits and exclusively distributing duty-free products from leading suppliers to airlines across Asia, North America, and South America.

As the years went by, ISG expanded its operations into the duty-free concession business, securing Pan American World Airlines as its very first customer in 1986. This significant achievement marked ISG as the sole airline duty-free concessionaire worldwide at that time, solidifying its position as an industry leader. From that date signing contracts with dozens of airlines till today.

With a rich legacy and an unparalleled commitment to excellence, ISG continues to revolutionize the airline industry. Our extensive network and exclusive partnerships ensure that passengers receive the finest duty-free offerings during their travels, elevating their overall experience.

Join us on this journey of innovation and unrivaled service. Explore our website to learn more about our remarkable history, our diverse range of duty-free products, and the exceptional value we provide to our airline partners and their passengers.
